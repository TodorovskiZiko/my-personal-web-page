My Education

I graduated from SUGS Orce Nikolov in Skopje, one of the most well-known high schools in the city. I finished with a perfect average grade of 5.00.

During my time there, I served as the Head of the IT Department, helping both students and teachers by fixing computers, setting up equipment, and solving technical problems.

I was also part of a startUp idea team, working on innovation and practical tech solutions. As a young innovator, I became a member of ImamIdea2 — a platform that helps startup teams connect with sponsors and financial support for their ideas.

These experiences helped me grow a lot—technically, but also personally. I traveled to different events, participated in activities, and made lifelong friendships that I will always treasure.

Now, I’m proud to be continuing my journey at the Faculty of Computer Science and Engineering - Skopje (FINKI). I’m focused and ready to be just as successful here, with a strong desire to learn, grow, and earn my degree in Software Engineering.
